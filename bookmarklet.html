SSD Sign — Bookmarklet

Tap while composing to sign your text without an extension.

Install

SSD Sign

Long-press the button above → tap Add bookmark. Name it SSD Sign and save. Done.

How to use

  1. Compose your post. Tap the text field to keep it focused.
  2. Open Bookmarks → SSD Sign.
  3. The sign page opens with your text. Tap Sign.
  4. Tap Copy token, then paste it back into your post.
